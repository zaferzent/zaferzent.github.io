date: 2026-05-03 01:00:00 +0300

For a while now, I have been sharing the photos I take on Flickr. One feature of Flickr really impressed me: it shows when the photo was actually taken.

So it does not only display the date the photo was uploaded to the site or app, but also the real time the photo was captured.

I think this is a great feature that should also exist on other photo sharing websites and apps.
