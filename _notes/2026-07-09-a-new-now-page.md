Today I added a new [Now](https://www.zaferzent.com/now) page to my blog.

The purpose of this page is to share what I'm currently working on, what I'm learning, and what I'm focused on at the moment. Instead of posting frequent updates on social media, I wanted a simple place where visitors can always see what I'm up to.

I also added a feature that automatically updates and displays the last updated date whenever I edit the page, so visitors always know how current the information is.

I plan to keep this page updated as my projects, interests, and priorities change over time.

This Now page will remain one of the most up-to-date pages on my blog, even when I'm not publishing new notes, articles, or photos. That's exactly what it's designed for—to reflect what I'm currently doing and focusing on. Because of that, I'll continue updating it regularly, regardless of how often I publish new content.
